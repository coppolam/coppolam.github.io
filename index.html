Designing robotic swarms


On-board relative localization for drones

The first step to collaboration is knowing where your neighbors are. This is still an ongoing challenge in robotics. In this project, we have developed on-board solutions that can fit on even the smallest drones, simply based on communication via antennas. This solution is light-weight and energy efficient. We demonstrated accurate results in indoor scenarios, where GPS and other such infrastructure is not available.
