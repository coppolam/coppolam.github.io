About me


I am an aerospace engineer and robotics researcher with international experience in large corporations as well as research laboratories, spanning multiple countries and cultures.

My strengths lie in my versatility and ability to devise efficient and effective solutions for complex problems. At the moment, my research interest is on the development of techniques to understand and design distributed robotic systems (a.k.a. robot swarms).

I am currently completing my PhD at Faculty of Aerospace Engineering the Delft University of Technology, where I am part of the Micro Air Vehicle Lab (MAVLab) as well as the Space Systems Engineering group.
