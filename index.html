About me


I'm a researcher in the field of robotics and artificial intelligence with a background in aerospace engineering. I am currently completing my PhD at the Delft University of Technology, where I am part of the Micro Air Vehicle Lab (MAVLab) as well as the Space Systems Engineering group.

Latest projects

Designing provable swarms

In this project, central to my PhD research, I set out to develop machine learning algorithms that would enable large teams of robots to collaborate together while verifying that their local choices would ultimately result in achieving the cooperative goal.


Read more

Relative localization in robot teams

How can we get small drones to sense each other during flight without using GPS? In this project, I have developed an efficient and effective technology for relative localization between robots in unknown scenarios, based only on antenna communication.
